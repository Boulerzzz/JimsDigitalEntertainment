This Privacy Policy describes how Jims Digital Entertainment Ab ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile games and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our mobile games, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, device identifiers, IP address
  • Game Analytics: Game progress, levels completed, scores, gameplay statistics
  • Technical Data: App crashes, performance data, feature usage
  • Advertising Identifiers: Mobile advertising IDs for personalized advertising

1.2 Information from Third-Party Services

Our games integrate with platform services that may collect additional information:

  • Google Play Games Services (Android): Leaderboards, achievements, player statistics
  • Game Center (iOS): Leaderboards, achievements, player statistics
  • In-App Purchase Platforms: Purchase history and transaction data

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact information to play our games.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our mobile games
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Analyze game usage and player behavior
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Send local push notifications (with your permission)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising and Personalization

Our games display advertisements through third-party advertising networks. These networks may use your device's advertising identifier to show you personalized ads based on your interests.

4.1 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Analytics Data: Typically retained for 26 months by Google Analytics
  • Advertising Data: Retention periods vary by advertising partner (typically 30-90 days)
  • Game Progress: Stored locally on your device and in platform services (Google Play Games/Game Center)

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 European Union Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectify inaccurate data
  • Right to erase your data (limited - see note below)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Important Note: Since we do not collect personally identifiable information and cannot identify individual users in our analytics systems, we cannot erase specific user data upon request. However, you can reset your advertising ID in your device settings to limit tracking, and uninstalling our games will remove locally stored data.

6.2 California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete your personal information (limited - see note above)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: We do not sell personal information. The same limitations regarding data deletion apply as mentioned in the GDPR section above.

6.3 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Push Notifications

Our games may send local push notifications to remind you about game events or updates. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve sending your data to our servers. You can disable these notifications in your device settings at any time.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our games and updating the "Last Updated" date above.
